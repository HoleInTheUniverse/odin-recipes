Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingridients

Dough

  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Toppings

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Steps

  1. Step 1: Make the dough
  2. Step 2: Knead dough on a floured surface
  3. Step 3: Divide in half and form into two tight dough balls
  4. Step 4: Make the pizzas
  5. Step 5: Preheat the oven, with a pizza stone on the lowest rack, to 550 degrees F (288 degrees C)
  6. Step 6: Prepare the first pizza
  7. Step 7: Lay 1/2 of the mozzarella on the crust, then sprinkle 1/4 teaspoon oregano and 1/8 teaspoon pepper over the top
  8. Step 8: With a quick back and forth jerk, make sure dough will release from the peel easily
  9. Step 9: Bake in the preheated oven until the crust begins to brown, 4 to 6 minutes
  10. Step 10: Slide the pizza onto the peel and remove from the oven
